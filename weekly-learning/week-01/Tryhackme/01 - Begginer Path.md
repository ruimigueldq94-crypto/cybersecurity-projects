## 🚀 **Getting Started with TryHackMe Machines**  
On **TryHackMe**, we learn by **starting and hacking virtual machines**.  
The **AttackBox** is a web-based machine used for conducting attacks on target machines.  

### 🛠️ **Steps to Set Up the Environment**  
1️⃣ **Start the AttackBox** by clicking the blue button at the top of the room.  
2️⃣ **Launch the target machine** and wait **1 minute** for configuration.  
3️⃣ **Copy the machine’s IP Address** and open **Firefox** (inside the AttackBox).  
4️⃣ **Paste the IP Address** in the URL bar and access the machine.
